01. Project Details

Tesla Outsourcing Services delivered a complete Scan-to-BIM Revit model at LOD 300 for a 300 m² heritage restaurant in Piccadilly, London.

The project leveraged high-resolution laser scans and historic façade imagery to create a digital twin. Our team faced the challenge of reconstructing two ornate façades, terrace upstands, chimneys, and intricate ground-floor doors and columns.

Using custom parametric window and door families, we filled in missing scan segments to generate accurate floor slabs and load-bearing walls. The result was a set of fully coordinated finished BIM deliverables produced within a strict ten-day turnaround despite tight site constraints.
